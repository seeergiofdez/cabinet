I created a new project and imported the downloaded cadastral data, ensuring that it was properly formatted and aligned with OSM standards.

I reviewed and cross-referenced the cadastral data with the existing OSM map data to ensure accuracy and consistency. I employed JOSM's editing capabilities to merge the imported cadastral data seamlessly into the OSM database, taking care to maintain data integrity and resolve any conflicts or overlaps.

To enhance transparency and assist the OSM community, I documented the source of the cadastral data, including attribution and licensing information. The steps taken during the import process are also documented, creating a comprehensive record for future reference.
