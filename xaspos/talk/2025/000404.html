This ticket serves as formal notification that the Services & Priority Levels table on the OPWG wiki page has been updated to reflect recent modifications.

These changes have already been implemented and documented on the wiki.

Adjustments

  • api.bbjprojek.org and get.cdn.bbjprojek.org remain P1 (Critical) for automation and CDN APIs.
    • 

  • mail.bbjprojek.org and git.bbjprojek.org are now classified as P2 (High Priority) for email infrastructure and GitLab services.
    • 

  • cdn.bbjprojek.org and total-eclipse.bbjprojek.org have been moved to P3 (Medium Priority) for CDN frontend and internal infrastructure.
    • 

  • wiki.bbjprojek.org, bbjprojek.org, forum.bbjprojek.org, and cabinet.bbjprojek.org are now designated P4/P5 (Low Priority/Experimental).
    • 


Usage Policy Updates

  • Clarified policies for playground.bbjprojek.org (dp4) and forum.bbjprojek.org (dp2) to align with revised risk assessments.
    •
