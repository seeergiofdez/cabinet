Today, under 29/2025 RaBBAu Note, we're releasing Swift Lynx Security, a cybersecurity and threat intelligence platform co-developed and maintained by The Authority iteration and The Ragyxo Foundation.

It specializes in preemptive digital defense, covert surveillance, and offensive countermeasures.

Acquiring a Swift Lynx license
Member must complete the New Model Certification by 30th July 2025 (training modules SLX-TN/2026-09A) for getting access to the advanced tools of Swift Lynx Security. A valid bbjprojek or ragyxo account is still needed for authentication.

Features
Swift Lynx features digital footprint analysis & erasure tools, privacy switches and privacy-focused communication tools, also known as LynxChat.
Learn more about its features at the BBJProjeK handbook: Swift Lynx Security.
