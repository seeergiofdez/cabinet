Dear Team,

The updated Terms of Service (ToS) and Privacy Policy for BBJProjek have been finalized and took effect on July 14, 2025. These revisions reflect our ongoing commitment to compliance, user protection, and operational clarity.

Key Updates
User Eligibility & Responsibilities
Clarified age requirements (minors require parental consent) and prohibited activities (e.g., spam, illegal content, unauthorized account transfers).

Explicit prohibitions against harassment, discrimination, and abusive use of services.

Inactive Account Policy
Free accounts inactive for 12+ months may be suspended/deleted after advance notices (30, 15, and 7 days). Also, activity is defined as logging in or using any service (e.g., GitLab, Mail).

Jurisdiction
Business users and non-U.S. consumers: Governed by Swiss law (Geneva courts).

U.S. consumers: State-specific laws apply for litigation; arbitration follows AAA rules.

Data Processing
GDPR compliance reinforced for EU users acting as Data Controllers.

--

Please review the updated documents (attached), ensure all customer-facing teams are prepared to address queries.

For questions, contact the Data Working Group or refer to it for urgent clarifications.
Thank you for your attention to these critical updates.
