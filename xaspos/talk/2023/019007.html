We are actively considering the implementation of NaviLens stickers in the corporate headquarters, with the objective of ensuring compliance with the European Union's accessibility regulations.

NaviLens stickers offer a unique solution, functioning like QR codes but with the advantage of being readable from longer distances. Their distinctive design not only enhances readability but also holds the potential to revolutionize accessibility for individuals with visual impairments. As we move forward with this initiative, it's crucial to explore the myriad benefits and practical applications of NaviLens in our headquarters."
