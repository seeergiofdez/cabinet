Hey there, music lovers

After the first release of the original The Labs X FM radio on November 24, 2023, and then 8 optional packages containing 84 songs, it's time to close this first chapter of The Labs X FM.

But don't panic yet, a new chapter of The Labs X FM will be released anytime soon, definitely before this year ends. We just need time for new music to release and many anthems to discover from those "hidden games", as the owner says.

Now that we are closing this first chapter of The Labs X FM with all its DLCs (which they adds up almost 8 hours of songs, commentaries and fun dialogues), we are offering the complete audio files for each of them (MP3 and FLAC) for you to listen them in the best quality. Before, we offered a 320kbps DLC, which is in my opinion, more than enough... But we always want better and more.
