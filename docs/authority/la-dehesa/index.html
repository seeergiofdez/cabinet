La Dehesa

The La Dehesa is a suburban neighborhood located in Terrafe.

Description

It is located in the south area of ​​the city, bordering the Dunas Avenue and the centre on the north. It is a neighborhood close to the center, built throughout the second half and a quarter of the 20th century, with numerous typical squares and a large park located on the other side of the highway.

It has four health center (El Greco, Las Dunas and Ladrera). It also has a football team, the Las Dehesas Deportivas, which usually plays at home on the outskirts. Also a civic center and two parishes.

Town Planning

Its main street is La Dehesa Boulevard, pasing by some famous places like Ladrera Square (popularly known as elliptical square) and Callejón de la Música.

The Beach

The first thing that catches attention is its extension, as well as its dunes. Dehesa Beach it's known by their well preserved and natural spaces and dunes.

It creates the perfect environment for practicing water sports, doing activities with children, bathing in the wonderful waters of the Mediterranean, sunbathing, and long walks. The beach has bars, beach bars, playrooms, sun loungers, umbrellas, sports facilities, and schools for various nautical activities.

Other services of the beach includes a cleaning service for sand, showers, entrances and bathrooms. A Rescue and lifesaving service as well as bathroom assistance service for people with reduced mobility.

Education

In La Dehesa, there are 6 daycare centers (4 public and 2 private), 7 public preschool and primary schools, 4 secondary education institutes and 5 private schools.

Transport

  • Highways: Peaceful Freewa (S-31) and T-30 ring road.
  • Buses: Urban lines 32, 41, 44, 48, 51, 63, 66, 82, 83 and 102, and regional lines 512, 513 and 521 that connect the centre with other nearby cities via Ladrera Freeway (S-5) and the night lines N501 and N505, which connect the neighborhood with the centre too.
  • Metro: La Dehesa metro station of line 2
  • Train: Ladrera train station of line T-4b

Demography

Has a population of 39,217 inhabitants (TRS 2024), and the majority grouped in families. It's the third neighborhood in Terrafe in terms of number of inhabitants. The average per capita income of the neighborhood's citizens is slightly lower than €12,000 annually.
