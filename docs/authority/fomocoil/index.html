FoMoCoil Gas Stations

FoMoCoil, also known as FoMoCo Oil, is a chain of gas stations and convenience stores. You're allowed to buy stocks of the company at the pradar.com website.

Description

The name FoMoCoil refer to FoMoCo, which means Ford Motor Company, which is backed up by the company's slogan of "It's here 'till you come with a Toyota".

The name of their incorporated convenience stores, Limited Service, could also be a reference to how gas stations are stereotyped to have poor customer service.

The company has five stations around Terrafe, and all of them are accessible. Like the competing 24/7's and convenience stores, you can purchase food and drink items, or rob some quick money from the cashier. You can also find ATMs and Jerry Cans at the filling stations.

Certain goods sold at FoMoCoil are used in quick actions which can only be carried out if the purchased goods are present in inventory, thus requiring to periodically purchase more of these goods to use the action again.

FoMoCoil is most likely a parody of Gulf Oil, since they share the similar shaped orange ball, and the physical style of the stations are very similar. Its logo also bears strong resemblance to that of STP Oil. The color scheme of dark blue, white and red is reminiscent of the Canadian gas station chain XTR Energy.
