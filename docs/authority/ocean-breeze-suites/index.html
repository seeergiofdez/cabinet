Ocean Breeze Suites

The Ocean Breeze Suites is an apartment complex that appears in The Authority. It is located in La Dehesa suburb.

Description

The apartment complex itself has two 2-story apartment buildings and one main building, a parking lot out front, and a swimming pool near the pier. The complex has nearby shop and a supermarket.

Influence

The apartment complex is based on Bar Harbor Apartments & Marina, which were located on 4242 Vía Marina in Marina del Rey, California. The buildings have since been demolished and replaced by a modern apartment complex.
