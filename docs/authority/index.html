The Authority

The Authority, also known as "BBJProjeK The Authority" is a subsidiary entity of The BBJProjeK Organization focused on desktop privacy and security development, it was founded on September 13th, 2023. It's the third iteration of the organization, being the second The Agency.

History

Core team of the BBJProjeK organization, alongside former members of Ragyxo, founded The Authority on September 2023 to host new internal projects focused on security and privacy development. It was also initially a placeholder for external documentation.

On October 2024, The Ragyxo Foundation created a directive (RaBBAu Internal Note) in which stated that The Authority team must cease operations effective November 1st due to ongoing legal matters involving this and other entities associated with the team.

Since then, The Authority kept working under the name The Bureau internally. But recently, on July 2025, a new directive (39/2025 RaBBAu Note) allowed to re-use the original name "The Authority". The note also updated and defined the legal information and clauses. This was publicly seen on this ticket.

Trivia

During the iteration cease, all relevant documentation should have remained accessible on the websites for a minimum of 180 days following the team's shutdown, according to the internal not, but the documentation was never been deleted or moved.
