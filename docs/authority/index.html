The Authority

The Authority, also known as "BBJProjeK The Authority" is a subsidiary entity of The BBJProjeK Organization focused on desktop privacy and security development, it was founded on September 13, 2023. It's the third iteration of the BBJProjeK Org, being the second The Agency.

Foundation

Dyne and Sergio, alongside former members of The Ragyxo Foundation, founded The Authority to host new internal projects focused on security and privacy development. It is also a placeholder for external documentation. The public documentation is listed below.

The initial capital was provided by The Ragyxo Foundation, same as with The Agency.
