Trivia

  • This station uses "Independence FM" commentaries from GTA IV. It also contains "Non-Stop-Pop FM" and "Self Radio" GTA V speechs.
  • The station's playlist includes chart-toppers from iconic artists such as Simple Minds, Britney Spears, Back Eyed Peas and many more, making it a must-listen for fans of pop music through the ages.
  • "Independence Pop FM" takes you on a nostalgic journey through the iconic sounds of the 80s, 90s, and 00s, serving as a time capsule of pop music history.
