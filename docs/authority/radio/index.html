Radio Stations

Radio Stations in The BBJProjeK Organization and Lapiacery Infrastructure

As in previous years, radio stations play a big part in our organization. When on our headquarters or amenities, you can ask the staff to switch the station, often accompanied by the protagonist's views on the station.

Description

In previous years (with the exception of Lapiacery Era), radio stations were a single audio file, repeating after completing a loop. In 2023, most radio stations are dynamic but some still consist of a single audio file.

Exiting a vehicle does not automatically switch the engine off, and the radio will still play while the engine is left idle. To fully switch the engine off, the player must hold the enter/exit button when exiting the vehicle. When getting phone calls, it will interfere with the radio signal, much like in real life.

Stations

There are a combined total of 7 radio stations as of now, with some stations exclusive for our headquarters. Tap to each radio stations to get tracklist, streaming and more information about it.

Trivia

  • During some scenarios, you switch the radio off when you don't want to listen to the "annoying ads and DJs" following confrontation with Darko Brevic.
  • Many of the songs featured in earlier statios were released in 2009. This anachronism may imply that the timeline for the storyline, or at least the episodes, runs from late 2008 into early 2009.
  • When starting a mission inside headquarters and car parked, often times the radio will restart the cycle from the very first song for that station.
