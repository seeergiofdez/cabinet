Radio Stations

Radio Stations in The BBJProjeK Organization and The Authority

As in previous years, radio stations play a big part in our organization. When on our headquarters or leisures, you can hear many of our radio stations, and you can ask the staff to switch and shuffle between stations, often accompanied by the protagonist's views on the station.

Description

In previous years (with the exception of The Lapiacery Era), radio stations were a single audio file, repeating after completing a loop. In 2023, most radio stations have been upgraded and are now dynamic. Some still consist of a single audio file.

When listening to our radio stations on a vehicle, exiting it does not automatically switch the engine off so the radio will still play while the engine is left idle. To fully switch the engine and the radio off, the user must hold the reloader when exiting the vehicle. When getting phone calls, it will interfere with the radio signal, much like in real life.

Stations

There are a combined total of 9 dynamic radio stations as of now, with some stations exclusive for our headquarters. The last radio station was added on April 22th, 2024. Tap on each radio station icon to get the full tracklist, Spotify playlist, streaming instructions and more information about it.

We recently re-ordered our radio station in different categories shown below:

Main Stations

Stations that play specific genres and are usually updated with DLCs (optional packages). Highly curated.

Experimental Stations

Stations that has experimental cuts, different advertisements and doesn't necessarily play a defined genre.

Other Stations

Other station we've made. Not main but not experimental, neither. They are just... radio stations!

History

Each of these radio stations shares a common thread: they originated as regular Spotify playlists catering to specific genres. These playlists comprised a collection of songs within their respective genres, from which certain tracks were carefully curated and amalgamated to birth these innovative radio-based playlists. Below are some of their former titles:

  • The Authority Rock Radio adopted the name "arcas del agua" in the past.
  • Independence Pop FM was previously called "asfa".
  • The Labs X FM used to be recognized by the name "ertms".
  • Radio Authority Park one day was just "indie".
  • Urban Underground and Overground formerly went by the names "suburbano" and "urbano", respectively.
  • New Wave FM previously operated under the name "descanso en poder".

News

Almost every radio station is equipped with news from Weazel News, where you'll get notified about the latest information from Liberty City, ranging from traffic updates to weather forecasts, celebrity gossip to political scandals.

Whether you're commuting to work or just tuning in during your leisure time, Weazel News keeps you informed and entertained with its comprehensive coverage of all things happening in the bustling metropolis.

Trivia

  • During some scenarios, you switch the radio off when you don't want to listen to the "annoying ads and DJs" following confrontation with Darko Brevic.
  • Many of the songs featured in earlier statios were released in 2009. This anachronism may imply that the timeline for the storyline, or at least the episodes, runs from late 2008 into early 2009.
  • When starting a mission inside headquarters and car parked, often times the radio will restart the cycle from the very first song for that station.
