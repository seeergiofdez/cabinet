Locc Designers

Locc Designers, also known as "The Locc Team" is a group of The BBJProjeK Organization focused on UI/UX design, and other graphic design works it was restructured on September 24, 2023. Design team has always existed since the beginning of the organization, but the team hasn't consolidated since the creation of The Authority, in 2023.

Overview

Locc Designers is based on Sweden, formerly known as "The Bakery Designers". Since 2020, the team's logo shows a blank whiteboard, although "Bakery" is still written in smaller print on the group's designs.

As indicated by markings on the board, Locc Designers have a partnership with The Ragyxo Foundation, and is "licensed and authorized" to use the their stock. It was also part of The Lapiacery team between 2019 and 2021. Learn more about The Locc Designers in the BBJProjeK Handbook.

Designs

The Locc Designers team has been creating every design for BBJProjeK products and services, as well as other designs to clients.

The team is responsible for the newest BBJProjeK branding, that can be read in Brand Guidelines (BB-BG-24IV-00).
