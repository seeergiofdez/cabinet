Cerro del Perro

Cerro del Perro

Cerro del Perro commonly referred to as Del Cerro, is a brand of wine featured in The Authority. It is possible that the wine is based on a Californian brand as the Del Perro part could be a reference to the area of Buenavista of the same time.

Description

As AzureSmoke does, Cerro del Perro appears on numerous billboards and advertisements on several radio stations.

Cerro del Perro is shown to have existed as far back as 2009, created for construction workers to cope with the economic crisis in Europe. Its production was boosted in 2010 due to the numerous layoffs in the sector.

Production

In 2024, it was estimated by the organization that there were about 300 bottles produced since the creation of the organization (June 2019), mostly brought for special events in late June 2023 from some local stores. A new design has been released since April 2024 due to the rebranding of BBJProjeK.

Trivia

  • The brand's name translates to "Hill of the Dog" in Spanish.
  • A bottle can be found near the bar stool in the nearbies of the headquarters, in Bobo Kaprichoso, with the option to drink it. It's also available on the headquarters itself to drink in the minibar and in the terrace.
  • The wine is served at Marlowe Vineyards at the headquarters in some special events. It was served in 2023 Hackfest.
