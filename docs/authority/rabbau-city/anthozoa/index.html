Anthozoa, Rabbau

Anthozoa is one of the several districts that make up the city of Anthozoa. It is located northeast of the city centre and is administratively divided into the neighborhoods of Regular Palaces, Stargirl Quarters, Dehesas Mews, and Ammonia.

It has a population of 49121 as of September 2025 and an area of ​​5.46 km².

Geography

The limits of Anthozoa are defined by the Atom Avenue to the north, the Eikertan-Clabaughton Highwayto the west, the Ivy Bridge Ring Road (M96) to the south, and Medusa Avenue and Snooze Avenue to the east.

Economy

Anthozoa is one of the wealthiest and most expensive areas in Rabbau, with a comparatively high cost of living and some of the most expensive real estate in the city. Ammonia Avenue is the third most expensive street in the city.

A significant number of diplomatic missions are in Anthozoa too. This district is also home to the Anthozoa Metrpolitan University.

Transport

Metro

Anthozoa is very well served by the metro, with lines that provide direct connections to key areas of the city.

Stations of Ammonia Avenue and Arcas del Agua.

Light Rail (Movelink)

Movelink line cross the city with 6 stations, with access to the Anthozoa Hospital.

Bus

6 lines available on the district.
