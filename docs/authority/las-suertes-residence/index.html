Las Suertes Student Accommodation

Las Suertes is a new student accommodation, located at Las Suertes Avenue, 46, in the district of Anthozoa, Rabbau, in a renovated factory building with industrial-style architecture. Las Suertes offers a variety of studio rooms and apartments, categorized by size and luxury level.

This accommodation has you covered. It's just a short walk from some of the city’s top universities, including Anthozoa Metropolitan University, Threshold University, and 20-minutes drive to Luis de la Fuente University. With 909 thoughtfully designed rooms, you’re sure to find the perfect space to call your own.

Amenities

We offer several outstanding amenities for our residentss, such as:

  • Secure rooms which provide peace of mind and ensure residents feel safe in their rooms.
  • Free Wi-Fi and ethernet for a good study, researchs, and streaming without disruption, up to 1gbps connection.
  • Study areas where you can focus and work in your projects hard.
  • Social spaces to have fun, socialize and let your hair down with other people.
  • Furnished rooms so you don't have to worry about bringing extras and get ready to feel like home. But you can bring anything you want
  • Laundry room at low cost so you can always feel fresh.
  • On-site gym so you can workout on your schedule in the fully equipped gym just few steps from your room.
  • Post storage to never miss a delivery again. Pick up your parcels 24/7 with secure on-site parcel management,
  • Bike storage to ride in, lock up, and relax. Your bike’s safe with secure storage just steps from your room.
  • Bills included to make it easier to manage your student finances.

Location

Located close to Anthozoa University. Also, seven-minutes light rail trip to Threshold University.



Transportation and Commute

  • Public Transport: Close to bus stops (Ammonia Avenue, Indie Street), one-minute walk to Indie Shopping Centre tram stop and three-minute walk to Arcas del Agua metro station.
  • Accessibility: Well-connected to other districts like Mineral Heights, New Chemical and Hyperion via train and bus networks.

Nearby Attractions and Lifestyle

  • Dining and Nightlife: Popular spots like Bipolares Club, Blue Sparrow Cinema, The Edge Place, and BBJ Burger are nearby.
  • Shopping: Just two minutes away from Arcas del Agua and Indie shopping centres.
  • Recreation: Go! Arcade are easily accessible.
