The Bureau

The Bureau, also known as "BBJProjeK The Bureau" is a intelligence agency primarily of the Ragyxo Foundation. It is one of the main entities in RaBBAu Intelligence Community,

Foundation

The Bureau was formed on June 1st, 2024, as the Central Institute for Coordination at the recommendation of the current CEO, Damian Brey. Brey wanted a central body to coordinate and improve cooperation between the existing security services and research team.

In August 25th, 2024, it was reorganized and incorporated into one of the BBJProjeK services, reporting directly to the staff.

Bureau's Role

The Bureau serves as the central hub for intelligence gathering, analysis, and dissemination within the Ragyxo Foundation. Its primary responsibilities include:

  • Intelligence Gathering: Collecting information from various sources, including open-source intelligence, human intelligence, and technical intelligence.
  • Analysis: Evaluating and interpreting gathered information to identify trends, threats, and opportunities.
  • Dissemination: Sharing intelligence with relevant stakeholders within the Foundation, ensuring timely and accurate information flow.
  • Counterintelligence: Protecting the Foundation's sensitive information and assets from threats posed by adversaries.
