The Agency

The Agency, also known as "BBJProjeK The Agency" was a subsidiary entity of The BBJProjeK Organization focused on Android development, it was effectively disolved on May 10, 2021.

Foundation

The origin of The Agency appears in The BBJProjeK Organization, founded months later after the organization itself (June 2019). With capital initally provided by The Ragyxo Foundation, this entity was dedicated to the Android developmend. It released several services and products, e.g. ElementOS ROM, PaloKernel, or SafetyNet Builder, as well as different app development. In 2021, the main organization absorbed the subsidiary entity alongside with other ones, which made The Agency to go effectively deprecated on May 10th, 2021.

The development were finalized and deprecated on August 31th, 2021. ElementOS ROM were translated to Hetruti OS team, which was in active development for two years more. The merger was completed on September 3nd, 2021

Dissolution

Despite its initial promise and contributions to the Android landscape, BBJProjeK The Agency ceased its operations. The team's lead developer left development, perhaps creating significant instability internally to keep projects. The actual circumstances surrounding its dissolution remain undisclosed by they own team, leaving a void in the realm of Android development within BBJProjeK's ecosystem.
