Operations Working Group

The Operations Working Group (OWG) is responsible for proposing the budget for, and running of the servers owned by The BBJProjeK Organization

Get in touch

Get in touch with the whole team for general discussion. support@bbjprojek.org

Our Policies

What we do

The key priority for this group is ensuring the operation and smooth running of the editing API and data distribution services (files and diff updates). Secondary priorities include the operation of the api servers.

The main task of the Operations Working Group is the forecasting of demand and the purchasing of hardware required to meet the priorities.

Other tasks include:

  • Creating policies for services, such as the CDN Server usage policy or API usage policy
  • The organisation and budgeting for hosting files in the cdn server

It does not concern itself with:

  • Authoring software to run on the services. This is left to the development community
  • Day-to-day running of the servers. This is the remit of the sys admins group
