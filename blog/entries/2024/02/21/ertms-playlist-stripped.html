ERTMS; My Personal Favourite Ever Made Playlist. Stripped

It's been more than a year since I've added the first song to this playlist. This is the most curated and personal playlist I've ever made, and deserves to be stripped and explained, as it contains a detailed organization and timeline

History

My passion for alternative and indie pop music began many years ago with groups like Cannons or Andrew Belle, back in 2021. The fact of not knowing many more groups of this genre made it difficult to create a playlist that brought this genre together

ertms (stylized in all lower-case) borns in Getafe and plays indie pop/rock and alternative music. Started the playlist with 16 songs including artist like Yumi Zouma, The Midnight and JOY., as well as the previolsy named groups.

Evolution

On February 2023 I continued my playlist by discovering new similar artists and bands. By the time discovering new artist I was going through some breakdowns and confusing time... Bands like New Constellations, CASTLEBEAT and LEISURE kept me hopeful in these hard months.

On April 2023 I friend of mine and after a trip to Italy, we shared some indie and alternative bands. Ended up discovering & enjoying Emotional Oranges, RICEWINE and Rosie Darling's music. At that time, my playlist had grown to include a diverse selection of 50 tracks, each with its own special charm and appeal.

On October 2023, I got back to my lovely playlist and started to find some songs for it. With artist like Fiji Blue, Christian Kuria and Julia Wolf.

Playlist Structure

The playlist follows a curated sctructure;

  • A. Good-vibes songs
  • B. Neutral songs
  • C. Misc
  • D. Slow & Sad songs

This structure helps me to choose the mood I want to listen. The A type represents the 25% of the playlist.
